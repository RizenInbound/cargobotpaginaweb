  • Why should I use CargoBot

    CargoBot helps to solve the problems that shippers and carriers face within the industry: inefficiency, unproductiveness, and lack of transparency in all transactions. With CargoBot your job becomes efficient and productive because the communication lines between shippers and carriers become direct, all documents are electronically stored, and invoices and rate of confirmationâ€™s are generated by our system. CargoBot provides you business with transparency. There are no hidden fees, all bids on shipments are visible by all carriers, carriers decide how they get paid and know exactly how much net income they will be making for each job they perform, and all carriers insurance and operating authority are stored within our system for shippers to have access to when working with the carrier.

  • Is CargoBot a loadboard?

    CargoBot is not a loadboard because with CargoBot carriers bid directly online for each job and that bidding is visible to all other carriers. Unlike loadboards, shippers negotiate directly with carriers. CargoBot simply provides the platform to allow both parties to meet but never gets involved in negotiations.

  • What is CargoBot?

    CargoBot is a free platform that helps connects shippers and carriers so that they may perform business directly with each other, without brokers. CargoBot allows you to complete all of your freight shipments in their entirety through our platform.
